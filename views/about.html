About Caleb

Hello! I'm Caleb, a designer and illustrator living in the great city of Philadelphia.

I have had the privilege to work with several amazing design teams including: Headcase Design, Pixar Animation Studios, and currently, Bluecadet. My work ranges from packaging, branding, interactive and everything in between. I have a deep love and appreciation of the outdoors which continues to inspire and inform my work.

If you have a small project, the next big thing, or just an itch to make some pretty cool stuff, I'd love to work with you. Please email me at heiseycb@gmail.com with your idea and let's talk it out.
